WifiIndicatorJS

WifiIndicatorJS is a simple program that displays your ping to both your router's control panel and to an external server. I made it because windows didn't accurately display when the wifi was working or not, and I also wanted to know my ping at all times. It uses ElectronJS to make a window, which is draggable, that is always on top.
